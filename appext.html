App Extractor


This privacy policy covers the use of the App Extractor application.

App Extractor does not collect any statistics, personal information, or analytics from our users, other than built in mechanisms that are present for all the mobile or embedded applications in their respective main distribution channels.
Cookies are not stored at any point by App Extractor, Google ads service may store cookies using its own mechanisms.
The developer DnG Software does not have access to any information stored on the users device.


App Extractor uses the following permissions:

  • 'read the contents of your storage', in to order to list apps that are installed to backup.
  • 'modify the contents of your storage', in order to save apps you wish to backup.
