DG Media Player

This privacy policy covers the use of the DG Media Player application.

DG Media Player does not collect any statistics, personal information, or analytics from our users, other than built in mechanisms that are present for all the mobile or embedded applications in their respective main distribution channels.
The DG Media Player application does allow for videos to be played via the network transport 'SMB/CIFS'.

Cookies are not stored at any point by DG Media Player, Google ads service may store cookies using its own mechanisms.
The SMB/CIFS authentication credentials can be stored optionally on the user's local device.
The developer DnG Software does not have access to any information stored on the users device.
